# Landing Page Specification: תוואי הצפון (Tavei HaTzafon)

---

## 1. General Brand Identity & UI/UX Style

**Brand Positioning:** Top-of-the-art, luxury, professional, and reliable.

**Visual Theme:** Minimalist yet bold. High-contrast typography — Serif for headings, clean Sans-Serif for body text.

**Background Animation:** Implement a subtle interactive parallax background. Floating, semi-transparent geometric shapes (triangles, squares, and architectural lines) should hover and move slightly in response to the user's scroll or mouse movement, symbolizing structure and engineering.

**Color Palette:**
- Deep Charcoals
- Slate Grey
- Gold or Industrial Orange accents (to emphasize the "Registered Contractor" status)

---

## 2. Page Structure (Sections)

### Section 1: Hero Section (כותרת עליונה)

- **Content Language:** Hebrew
- **Main Heading (H1):** בונים לכם בית, בראש שקט.
- **Sub-heading:** תכנון וביצוע פרויקטים בסטנדרט הגבוה ביותר על ידי קבלן רשום. אנחנו לוקחים אחריות על כל שלב בדרך – מהיסודות ועד המפתח.
- **Call to Action (CTA):**
  - Primary Button: `תיאום פגישת ייעוץ בשטח`
  - Secondary Button: `קבלו הצעת מחיר ראשונית`
- **Visuals:** High-resolution video background of a luxury finishing project or a clean architectural render.

- **Gallery:** High-quality "Before & After" image slider.
- **Testimonials:** Floating quote cards.

- **Marketing Tip:** Include a "Registered Contractor" badge logo near each project title.

---

### Section 5: Contact Us & Lead Magnet (יצירת קשר)

  - טלפון (Phone)
  - סוג פרויקט (Project Type): New Construction / Renovation

## 3. Technical & Marketing Requirements

### Sticky Header
The logo of **תוואי הצפון** and the "Registered Contractor" symbol must remain visible at all times while scrolling.

### Video Integration
Integrate short "vlog-style" clips of Ariel on-site to build immediate trust and personal connection.

### Bureaucracy Clause
Add a small section or tooltip with the following message:

> אנחנו מטפלים בבירוקרטיה מול הרשויות והאדריכלים
> *(We handle the bureaucracy with the authorities and architects for you.)*

### Footer
- Links to social media profiles
- Official contractor license number
- Quick contact button for **WhatsApp**
